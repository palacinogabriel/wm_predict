How this works

Qualifying third-place groups — After the group stage, the best third-placed team from each of the 12 groups advances. Only 8 of the 12 third-place teams qualify for the Round of 32. They are ranked by points, then goal difference, then goals scored. The 8 groups whose third-place team ranks highest are the qualifying third-place groups (e.g. A, B, C, E, F, G, H, K).

Third-place mapping combo — FIFA's Annex C defines exactly which slot in the Round of 32 bracket each qualifying third-place team fills. The assignment depends on which combination of groups provides the 8 third-place qualifiers. The combo string is a lookup key into that table.
